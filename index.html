Features

Nature is the ultimate source of our living. It includes both living and non-living things and maintains the ecosystem. Plants, animals, and humans all depend on nature for their survival. It provides oxygen, sunlight, soil, water, and many other essential elements. Everything around us — from the sun and moon to trees and animals — is part of nature. Every element in nature is interconnected. Humans depend on it for food, water, medicine, and shelter. Hence, preserving nature is crucial for a healthy, balanced life.
